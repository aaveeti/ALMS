This week's ALMS support group task is to either develop last week's text by revising and rewriting, or alternatively, write a short (1 page) text about a different topic or a question relevant to your studies (+ peer reviews). I chose to write about a new topic and wrote an essay on cybersecurity focusing on the threat posed by the human factor. I also made an observation: my course material in Cyber Security Base uses cyber security (two words), while other media uses cybersecurity (one word). So I did some research and found that both are equally correct in terms of meaning. However, the two-word variant is more common in British English and in older texts, whereas the one-word variant is more common in American English. The one-word variant is also often seen as more modern, so I will probably start using that one more often.
