My plan for the course

My plan for the course is to document what I have learned and the hours I have spent on this website. After completing all the mandatory tasks, I have 98 hours remaining, which I plan to use as follows:

  • I want to read at least one book about a new programming language or computer architecture in English.
  • I will practice programming exercises in English and write summaries of what I have learned.
  • I will join the course groups and attend their sessions.
  • I will socialize in English with my friends online and in real life.
