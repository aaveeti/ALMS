Overview of the week

Reflection

This first week was mainly about getting started and setting foundations for the course. I spent time attending lectures, completing the first compulsory tasks, and planning my website. In addition to my course work, I also took an active step towards improving my English oral skills by using it during online gaming sessions, which felt pretty natural considering I have already been doing it for the majority of my life. Time management went fairly well this week, although juggling around 4 courses is pretty exhausting.

Apart from my school work I did two shifts at work this weekend. I work at the airport which is a very international environment and where English is used frequently. I have to confirm from my counsellor if I can use some of my work hours for this course. The first counselling meeting is next week.
